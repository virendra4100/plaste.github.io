About Us

Our Product

We are one the leading manufacturers and suppliers of Artex Gypsum Plaster. It is prepared from calcium sulfate dihydrate. It is one of the main ingredient in drywall and is found is most interior mixes. This is widely used as a fertilized in mould, sculptures and also as a plaster material. Artex Gypsum Plaster is very light in weight and can be applied directly on brick, solid and hollow block.
